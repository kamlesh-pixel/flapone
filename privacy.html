How Cookies Are Used?

A cookie can be defined as a small file that asks permission to be placed on the computer's hard drive. Upon agreeing, the file is added, and the cookie helps in analyzing web traffic or lets you know when you visit a specific site. Cookies allow the response of web applications to you as an individual. The web application can modify its operations as per your requirements, likes, and dislikes by gathering and remembering information about your preferences.

We use traffic log cookies for the identification of which pages are being used. This is good for us in examining data about webpage traffic and improving the website to tailor to customer needs. This information is only used for statistical analysis, and post that the data is removed from the system.

Overall, cookies can give a better experience on the website by allowing us to monitor which pages are useful to you and which are otherwise. A cookie does not give us access to your computer or any information, other than the data you choose to share with us.

You can accept or decline cookies. Most browsers accept cookies automatically, but the settings can be altered to decline cookies. But, then you may not be able to explore the full potential of this website.

We do not sell, distribute, or lease your information to third parties unless we have permission or are needed by law. The personal information can be used to send promotional information about third parties which you may find interesting if you permit the same.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most commonly asked questions about our courses and features.

What are the eligibility criteria for CPL ground classes?

Students must be at least 17 years old and have completed 10+2 with Physics and Mathematics as subjects. A Class 2 Medical Certificate from a DGCA approved doctor is also required before joining.

How long is the CPL ground course?

Our comprehensive CPL ground course typically takes 3-4 months to complete, with classes held 5 days a week. The exact duration may vary based on student progress and batch size.

What is your batch size?

We maintain small batch sizes of maximum 15 students to ensure personalized attention and better learning outcomes. This allows our instructors to focus on individual student needs.

Do you provide study materials?

Yes, we provide comprehensive study materials including textbooks, practice question banks, and digital resources. Our materials are regularly updated to align with the latest DGCA exam patterns.

What is the fee structure and are there installment options?

Our fee structure is competitive and we do offer installment payment plans. We also provide guidance on aviation education loans available through our banking partners. Please contact us for the latest fee details.

Do you provide placement assistance after completion?

Yes, we provide career counseling and placement assistance. Our industry connections with various airlines and our alumni network help students find opportunities after successful course completion.
