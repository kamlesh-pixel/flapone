About Us

FLAPONE AVIATION PRIVATE LIMITED

At Flapone Aviation, our mission is to make aviation and drone technology accessible, practical, and future-ready. We are dedicated to nurturing the next generation of commercial pilots and drone experts through world-class training, cutting-edge equipment, and a learning-first ecosystem. With a strong focus on safety, innovation, and quality, we empower every enthusiast and professional to soar higher and lead with confidence in the aerial space.

We are dedicated to nurturing the next generation of commercial pilots and drone experts through world-class training, cutting-edge equipment, and a learning-first ecosystem.

Welcome to "FLAPONE AVIATION PRIVATE LIMITED", a premier Remote Pilot Training Organization (RPTO) approved by the Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA), Government of India, to conduct Drone Pilot Training in Delhi NCR. We are also recognized as a leading AvTech start-up by the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade, offering a comprehensive range of aviation courses and consulting services.

 

Vision

Our vision at "FLAPONE AVIATION PRIVATE LIMITED" is to enrich the lives of individuals aspiring to build a career in the drone & aviation industry. We strive to empower our students with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel in this dynamic field.

 

Mission

At "FLAPONE AVIATION PRIVATE LIMITED", our mission is to provide quality and affordable training solutions under one roof, enabling our students to soar to new heights in their careers. We are dedicated to meeting the evolving needs of the aviation industry by delivering exceptional training experiences and fostering a culture of excellence.

Flapone Aviation Receives the Award for Best Pilot and Drone Training Institute

There are rapid developments in the world of aviation and unmanned aerial vehicles, or drones as they are famously called. Training institutes play a vital role in people becoming experts in aviation and drone piloting. Recently, Airborne Aviation Academy, which is now Flapone Aviation, has emerged as a trustable name in the field of aviation education in India, received the award for the Best Pilot and Drone Training Institute. This award is a result of their excellence in preparing professionals for the ever-evolving aerospace industry.
